# Market Research Survey

## What it's for
A survey sent to basketball academy owners to collect market data and validate the FullControl concept. This is not an onboarding survey for customers — it's a research instrument. The FullControl prototype is embedded in the survey so owners can react to what they're seeing.

## Who's working on it
Zoran

## Current status
Scrapped. The survey was distributed and data was collected. It will be reworked once FullControl has active users to survey.

## End goal
A redesigned, targeted survey that collects feedback from real FullControl users — not just prospective ones.

## Core blockers
- Needs active users before it can be meaningfully reworked

## How it connects to other projects
- **prototype/** — embedded in the survey so respondents can see the product
- **business/** — survey findings feed into the market data explainer and investor materials
- **Supabase** — response data is stored there
