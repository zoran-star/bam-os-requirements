# Sales Conversation Agents

## What it's for
System prompts and building materials for the AI sales agents that run inside FullControl's GHL automations. A lead texts in — the agent qualifies them and books a trial without any human involvement. This is the "AI-native" layer that makes FullControl feel autonomous.

## Who's working on it
Zoran

## Current status
Booking agent is being built for BAM GTA. Closing AI (post-trial conversion) and Rebooking AI (no-show re-engagement) are currently being designed in whiteboard sessions SES-025 and SES-026. Zoran is figuring out a way to get the training data of all of the best sales academies that BAM Business works with to train the agent. Still in early stage of development. Zoran and Danny have aligned on training process.

## End goal
Fully autonomous lead-to-member conversion with no human involvement required in FC. Every stage of the sales funnel — first contact, trial booking, post-trial follow-up, re-engagement — handled by an AI agent.

## Core blockers
- Closing AI and Rebooking AI still in design phase

## How it connects to other projects
- **bam-gta-phase1/** — the booking agent is live inside BAM GTA's GHL automations
- **GoHighLevel** — agents run inside GHL automation workflows
- **Notion** — placeholder variable values come from the Onboarding Data Points DB
