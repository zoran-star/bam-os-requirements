Never use em dashes (— or &mdash;) in any copy, HTML, or text output.

**Why:** Zoran explicitly dislikes them and has corrected this multiple times.

**How to apply:** Replace with a period, comma, or rewrite the sentence. No exceptions anywhere in the project.
