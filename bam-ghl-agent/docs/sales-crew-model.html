03

✅ Confirm agent

Works leads who've booked a trial. Kicks off with a fixed initial automation — the same confirmation text/email to everyone the moment they book. The instant the lead replies to any of it, that automation switches off and the agent takes over (replies, logistics, "can't make it"). The lead stays in the Scheduled Trial stage until the post-trial form is filled — that's when they leave and the agent turns off.

👻 Ghosted — an automation, not an agent

Aggressive + short. A ghosted lead isn't replying, so there's nothing to "converse" with — it's just timed nudges into silence. An adjustable automation, not an agent. The second they reply → straight back to Booking. If they're still silent after the nudges → they roll down into the 💔 Lead Nurture (the sparse long game) — they're not given up on, just touched less often.

⚠️ Redesign needed: today this runs as a rigid GHL workflow — rebuild it as a portal automation so it's editable in the Brain + observable, then switch the GHL one off.

07

💔 Lead Nurture — the long game

The catch-all for everyone who didn't convert — fed by two streams: leads who ran out of 👻 Ghosted (still silent) and anyone marked Lost at any stage (Booking / Confirm / Closing) who isn't 🚫 Unqualified. Not given up on — just touched sparingly (odd email or text over weeks → months). One-way, so an automation, not an agent. Any reply → Booking with context. Exact cadence = you'll define the first one.

⚠️ To build: the whole Lead Nurture automation — the sparse cadence, email + text sends (via Resend), the unqualified gate, and routing every non-unqualified Lost lead in here. Nothing exists yet.

⚠️ To build — the whole Resend email system: a shared foundation for every email send (Lead Nurture, Ghosted email, the Confirm/Closing initial automations). Needs: verify a sending domain + DNS (SPF/DKIM/DMARC), an api/_email.js send wrapper, templates, and a delivery/bounce webhook. (Resend's already wired for password-reset emails — extend that.)
