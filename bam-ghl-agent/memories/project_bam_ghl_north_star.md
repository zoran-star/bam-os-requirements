The north star for bam-ghl-agent is **a single portal serving both clients and staff** for BAM Business.

**Clients can:**
- Interact with staff in dedicated chat windows
- See every ad campaign they're running and adjust their own ad spend
- Submit support tickets and check on their systems

**Why:** Earlier framing called the north star "an autonomous GHL agent that augments staff." That's a *capability* inside the portal, not the destination. The destination is the portal itself — a self-serve surface for clients plus an operations surface for staff, sharing one backend.

**How to apply:**
- When describing the project, lead with the dual-portal vision (client + staff in one product), not "autonomous agent."
- The agent capabilities (drafting fixes, building assets) are features that live *inside* the portal, on the staff side.
- Staff side = bam-portal (already exists, being reconnected).
- Client side = currently the customer-facing HTML files (client-portal.html, onboarding flow), which will eventually consolidate into a real authenticated client portal with chat, ad campaign visibility, and ticket status.
- Don't treat client-portal.html as just a support form — it's the seed of the client-side surface.
