🤖 Training Your Sales Agent

How the booking agent works, and how to train it until you trust it — for leads in the Responded stage.

The one rule: you train the agent in 4 places, but they all feed one shared brain. Teach it anywhere → it's smarter everywhere. Nothing it sends goes out without your approval (for now).

🛠️ The 4 ways you train it

📝

Brain editor

Edit what it knows + how it acts, by section (tone, pricing, objections…). Saved → used on the next reply.

💬

Sandbox chat

Text it like a parent. Watch it reply with its reasoning + confidence. Hit 📝 teach to correct it, ⭐ save a great reply as an example.

🧪

Break-it tests

~38 tricky scenarios (discount traps, made-up facts, jailbreaks…). Run each, mark ✅ held up / ❌ broke, fix the brain, re-test.

🤖

Live approvals

Real replies wait for you in the Inbox → Bot tab. Confirm, or adjust + save your change as a learning.

🔁 The training loop

  1. Edit the Brain — set tone, pricing, objections, guardrails. (Sandbox → 📝 Brain tab)
  2. Chat-test in the Sandbox — play the parent. When it's off, hit 📝 teach. When it's perfect, ⭐ save as an example.
  3. Run the Break-it lab — try the tricky cases. Every ❌ tells you which brain section to fix (the 📍 chips show where it pulled from).
  4. Go live with approvals — real leads' replies show in Inbox → 🤖 Bot. Confirm good ones; adjust the rest and save the fix as a learning.
  5. Keep teaching — every correction (sandbox OR live) sticks to this academy's brain and improves all future replies.

✅ What "good" looks like

Before you lean on it, drive the Break-it board to all ✅ and make sure live drafts feel right 9 times out of 10. Watch the confidence % and the 📍 source chips — low confidence or a wrong source = a brain section to tighten.

It will never guarantee zero mistakes — that's why every message is human-approved at this stage.

📍 Where everything lives

Staff portal → /sandbox      💬 Chat · 📝 Brain · 🧪 Break it
Client portal → Inbox → 🤖 Bot   approve / adjust real replies
Client portal → Business Blueprint → Agent learnings  (what it has learned)

🔒 It only ever replies to leads in the "Responded" stage — never members, never other stages. And it texts you a heads-up: a digest every 2 hours of how many chats are waiting, plus a ping each time a new one needs approval.

🧠 The brain = layers + lessons + examples

Every reply is built fresh from these, combined:

🌐 General — BAM owns · every academy inherits
how it sells: tone, objections, flow, guardrails
📍 Location — this academy
address, schedule, coaches, proof
🎁 Offer — the product
program, pricing, policies, who qualifies
🎯 Goal — the objective
follow-up cadence · book a free trial
🧠 Lessons + ⭐ Examples — your corrections
stay with this academy unless BAM promotes a general one

🧩 The pieces under the hood

🔔 The Spine   GHL pings us the moment a lead replies, so the system knows. Foundation for everything.

📨 Pipeline button   "Summer Special" — hand-picks a lead into the nudge automation.

🤖 Approval queue   finds Responded-stage leads needing a reply, drafts one with the brain, waits for your OK.

🛡️ Guardrails   if it's unsure, off-topic, a complaint, or out of its knowledge → it flags to a human instead of guessing.

🚦 What's live vs. coming

✅ live   spine · sandbox + brain editor · break-it lab · summer-special button
✅ live   approval queue (human approves every send) · Responded-stage only
✅ live   agent learnings (client tab + staff manage) · reminder texts (2h digest + per-chat)
⏳ later the agent acting on its own (book / follow-up / escalate tools)
Why human-approved first? A booking agent that talks to real leads has to be trusted. We train it in the sandbox, prove it on the break-it board, then let it draft live — but a person approves every message until it's earned full autonomy.
