What changed Pipeline Booking Confirm Closing All moves Open decision

Shipped today (your 2026-07-08 calls)

"Abandon" is now "Unqualified" on every Hawkeye button.
Booking follow-up nudges are gone - a quiet lead now always comes back as a "Send to Ghosted" proposal.
Confirm reminders live in the step config (gear), not in Hawkeye - approve the templates once, they send on their own.
Done Trial has zero automations now - the post-trial form (trainer note + optional sign-up link + notes for the agent) is the only preplanned touch. The scripted 3-step sequence is retired.
"Ready to enroll" is just a reply with the link - the sign-up link is embedded in the editable draft, one Approve & send.
Every agent can mark Unqualified - the dead-end button is now on every Confirm and Closing card too (opp closed + unqualified tag, no message, no nurture).
Lost after 3 - the closing agent never calls a quiet lead lost itself; the follow-up loop raises the Lost card after 3 unanswered follow-ups.

On every card, every agent

Edit anything, teach why (required) - changing the agent's guess (a draft, a plan message, a booking detail) flips the button to "Confirm edits and ..." and the teach-why note becomes MANDATORY before it sends. The note is saved as a lesson and applied to the next draft.
6-second undo - every send and every move holds for 6s with an Undo pill before it commits.
Quiet hours - approvals outside 8:00am-9:30pm get scheduled for the morning, not fired at midnight.
Tap the thread - opens the full conversation history behind any card.
