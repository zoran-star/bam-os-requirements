Common questions

  • Can't sign in? Use the "Forgot password?" link on the login screen to reset your password by email.
  • Not getting notifications? Make sure notifications are enabled for FullControl in your device's system settings.
  • Need to add a teammate? Open the Team tab and choose "Invite teammate."
  • Something looks wrong? Use the feedback button inside the portal, or email us directly.
