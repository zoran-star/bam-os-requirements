Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: July 3, 2026

What data we hold

When you message a business we serve on Facebook Messenger or Instagram, or interact with a business through our platform, we may store: your name or username as shared by Meta, the messages you exchanged with that business, and related contact details you provided (such as a phone number or email address).

How to request deletion

  1. Email zoran@byanymeansbusiness.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include the name of the business you messaged and the Facebook or Instagram account you contacted them from, so we can locate your records.
  3. We will confirm receipt within 7 days, delete your personal data and message history from our systems within 30 days, and email you confirmation once complete.

Deletion covers all personal data we hold about you: message threads, contact records, and any profile information received from Meta. Data we are legally required to retain (for example, payment records) is retained only as required and never used for any other purpose.

Removing our access on Meta's side

You can also remove the connection between your account and our app directly in your settings:

  • Facebook: Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites → remove the app.
  • Instagram: Settings → Website permissions → Apps and Websites → remove the app.
Questions? Contact zoran@byanymeansbusiness.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
