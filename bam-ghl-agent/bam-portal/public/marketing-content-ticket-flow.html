02
How a client opens a ticket - 5 entry points

Three create content tickets; two go straight to marketing.
Marketing tab · wizard
+ Add New Campaign
6-step wizard: pick offer → guide → upload assets → budget → landing page → Submit. Bundles everything into one "mega" ticket.
→ content_ticket source: new-campaign
Campaign detail
+ Add New Creative
"Input assets" modal - drop footage/photos or a share link, pick format, add captions + notes for an existing campaign.
→ content_ticket channel: ads
Organic view
+ Request a new creative
Same modal, organic flavour - the finished post comes back to the client to approve. Only when Organic content is ON.
→ content_ticket channel: organic
Spend bar · pencil
Change monthly spend
New monthly spend + reason. No creative work, so it skips content entirely.
→ marketing_ticket type: budget
Creative tile · ✕
Delete a creative
"Remove this creative" goes straight to marketing - nothing to produce, just pull it down.
→ marketing_ticket type: remove
Priority: any create flow can be flagged ⚡ urgent → High priority (3-business-day SLA) vs Normal (5-day). Rides along into the staff board's sort.

07
Content → Marketing handoff (and back)

"Send to Marketing" is the bridge. What it does depends on whether the ticket was a fresh request or a revision round-trip.

Fresh spawn

Content ticket has no marketing link yet → INSERT a new marketing_ticket:

status: in-progress files = the finals type from source assigned to the SM pings Cam pings Ximena + due date

The content ticket then closes → completed. Campaign-create tickets carry the offer, monthly spend + landing page across.

Ximena's ping lands in #content-marketing: ➡️ Sent to marketing - Content [XXX] ready to launch. ⚡ URGENT Due Tue Jun 30. @Ximena - so she sees the work and her deadline the moment it lands. Fires on revision round-trips too.

Revision round-trip

Marketing earlier hit ↩ Request Content Revision, which spawned this content ticket (linked via marketing_ticket_id).

Sending it back UPDATES the original marketing ticket in place - new finals replace the files, awaiting_revision clears, and it re-enters marketing's Active tab. No duplicate ticket.

◆ One-line summary

  • Content ticket = make the creative (Cam/Eli).
  • Marketing ticket = launch & run the ad (Ximena/SM).
  • Paid work flows content → marketing; organic loops content ↔ client; budget & remove go straight to marketing.
  • Request Client Action pauses either side until the client responds, all via their own Slack channel.
