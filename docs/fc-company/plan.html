FullControl

The AI-Powered Command Center for Sports & Fitness Businesses

01

Executive Summary

FullControl is an AI-powered command center for sports and fitness businesses. It replaces the fragmented systems operators rely on today — CRM, scheduling, payments, marketing, analytics, and operations — with one unified platform that helps them run their business with clarity, confidence, and control.

But FullControl is not just about consolidating tools. It is built on a core belief:

The people who are best at relationships, communication, culture, and impact will be the most successful in the AI era — once they have the right systems behind them.

Today, many of the most talented coaches and operators are limited not by their craft, but by the complexity of running a business. They entered this space to develop people, build communities, and create impact — not to manage software stacks or learn technical systems.

As a result, they are forced to operate within fragmented tools that are difficult to use, disconnected from one another, and require constant manual effort.

FullControl solves this by turning a collection of tools into a single intelligent system. It allows operators to:

  • Understand what is happening across their business
  • Identify what matters most
  • Receive clear guidance on what to do next
  • Take action quickly

And over time: interact with their business through simple conversation.

You should be able to talk to your business — and it runs.

FullControl begins with sports training facilities and fitness studios — a category with high operational complexity, strong recurring revenue, and clear demand for better systems — and expands deeply within this ecosystem.

The opportunity is not to build another software tool. It is to build the operating system for how this entire industry runs.

From tools → to intelligence → to autonomy

Core Product Concept

FullControl is not just software. It is a command center, a decision engine with ever-improving intelligence, a system of record and system of action, and an operator co-pilot.

The defining interaction model is:

You can talk to your business, and it acts for you.

> “Why are conversions down?” Concise analysis of what changed and why
> “Follow up with all unconverted trials” Conversational AI handles outreach and recapture
> “Fix my low-performing ad content” Analyzes ads, benchmarks against industry, produces new creative
> “Cancel Johnny’s membership and send an exit interview” Executes both actions with no manual work
> “What should I focus on today?” Scans the entire business for highest-leverage tasks
Insight Recommendation Execution Autonomy Freedom

Go-To-Market

Phase 0 — Beta Testing

Internal & Consulting Validation

Test with internal By Any Means academies for high-volume feedback, then expand to consulting clients across team sports (soccer, volleyball, baseball), individual sports (martial arts, track and field), and fitness (yoga, pilates, boutique gym). Ensures adaptability before market launch.

Phase 1 — Months 0–6

Early Basketball Adoption

Leverage existing network and a shotgun launch to reach 100–200 basketball academies in the first 90 days. After 3 months, begin testing traction in other team sports.

Phase 2 — Months 6–18

All Sports Academies

Choose 2–3 team sports to create more content around, find partners, sponsor events. Goal: 300 non-basketball academies by Month 12.

Phase 3 — Months 18+

Fitness Adoption

A more catered approach to fitness studios, leveraging the proven product and expanded operator network.
