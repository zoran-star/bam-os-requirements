Honest answers

Questions we know
you're thinking

We've heard these before. Here's how we think about them.

❓ “Isn't this just GoHighLevel for gyms?”

GHL is a horizontal platform that's notoriously complex. FullControl is AI-native, vertical-first, and radically simpler. It's the difference between Salesforce and Toast — same category, completely different product philosophy.

❓ “The market is too niche.”

Basketball is the wedge, not the ceiling. Sports training → fitness → service businesses. Same playbook as Toast (restaurants → food service) or ServiceTitan (HVAC → all trades). Start narrow, expand with the platform.

❓ “Why can't incumbents just add AI?”

Bolting AI onto a bloated legacy platform creates a worse experience, not a better one. FullControl is AI-native from day one — the architecture, UX, and data model are all built around intelligence. You can't retrofit that.

❓ “How do you know people will pay?”

We don't just think they will — we've already been consulting for 20+ academy operators. They're paying $500+/mo for fragmented tools that don't work together. Consolidating into one system at $300/mo is a no-brainer.
