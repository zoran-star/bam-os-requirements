## 1. The two doors

```
🟢 PRODUCT DOOR — FullControl              🔵 PARTNER DOOR — BAM Business
   All-in-one software: ops + marketing       Human scaling manager + Growth Share
   You / AI run it.  Tiered, scalable.         FullControl is the cockpit they use.
   Founding price $499, moves up.              Base + 10-15% of growth above baseline.
   → software-like margin                      → premium, aligned, UNCAPPED ACV
```

Two clean choices for the buyer: **"give me the product"** vs **"be my growth partner."** Maps directly to the positioning doc (ops-as-a-service + AI, operator stays in command).

**Why growth-share lives in BAM Business, not bare FC:** people only give away upside when they feel a human partner in the business with them. The human de-risks the model and makes attribution clean.

## 2. The tiering axis

Tier on the things that scale both value **and** cost-to-serve:

- **Automation depth** — how many AI agents / how much runs itself
- **Human involvement** — self-serve → done-with-you → growth partner
- **Size as a multiplier** — a 100-client academy pays more than a 40-client one on the same tier

**Key realization:** the live product is already worth ~$1,000 (booking agent, full inbox, Meta marketing + KPIs, member billing, pipeline, onboarding, app). **$499 is a deliberate founding land-grab on a Growth-tier product, not the ceiling.**

## 3. The tier ladder

### 🟢 FC Core — entry (~$499 founding → ~$699 standard)
*The command center. One place to run the academy + AI books your trials.*
- Client portal: Home/KPI dashboard, Sales board + pipeline, Members + Stripe billing (pause/cancel/refund), Contacts, Inbox (SMS + email), Support tickets
- **Booking Agent** (AI converts leads to trials, Hawkeye-approved)
- Marketing **tools**: Meta campaign management + ad performance dashboard (results) + CAC/KPIs
- Onboarding wizard + Action Items, mobile app, owner notifications

### 🟢 FC Growth — the flagship (~$899-999, **$499 founding**)
*Everything in Core + the full crew so the business runs itself. Where most live clients actually sit.*
- **Full sales crew: Confirm + Closing agents**
- **Follow-up nudge engine** + Ghosted→Nurture automation
- **AI marketing insights**
- **Agent brain editor + lesson library + training sandbox** ← the "more it learns" moat
- Organic content pipeline, Mass send, Website enrollment funnel (self-serve join→pay)

### 🟢 FC Scale — premium (~$1,499+)
*For bigger / multi-location academies and power users.*
- Multi-location support + location switching, multi-user team + permissions
- Advanced analytics: funnel view, industry benchmarks, activity ledger, custom KPIs (near-term)
- Resources library, priority support, higher member/SMS/AI volume, light done-with-you

### 🔵 BAM Business — partner door (Base + 10-15% of growth above baseline)
*Not a software tier. A human scaling manager runs growth WITH them. FC is the cockpit.*
- Everything in FC Scale + dedicated scaling manager
- Growth-share lives here (de-risks the "give away upside" fear)
- Where ACV goes uncapped

## 5. Founding 25 — terms

- **Cap: 25 net-new academies** (stretch 30 only if onboarding holds without cracking)
- **Window: ~90 days** or until full, whichever first
- **Price: $499 early-access** (FC Growth value), grandfathered for founders
- **Target raise after founding closes:** ~$799-999 standard
- **Scarcity + proof** drive signups; small-and-flawless beats big-and-broken (protect the BAM brand halo)

Watch 3 numbers to prove the low-price trade is working: **total gross profit $ ↑**, **cost-to-serve per account ↓**, **net revenue retention >100%**.

## 6. Rollout sequence

```
STEP 1            STEP 2                          STEP 3
🏀 BAM GTA     →  🔄 Existing BAM academies     →  🚀 Founding 25
   (dogfood)        + BAM Business clients           (net-new external)
   prove on own     convert as UPGRADE               founding $499 deal
   academy          (same price, more value)         cap 25, ~90-day window
                    = the proof machine
                    
   GATE: ─────────► "V2 done + it's an app" ◄───────── critical path
```

**Two pricing motions — keep them separate:**
- **Step 2 (existing clients):** upgrade language. *"Same price, more product."* No founding pitch.
- **Step 3 (Founding 25):** the founding offer. Scarcity + cap + deadline.

**Steps 1-2 are the launch engine for step 3** — capture proof (testimonials, before/after metrics) relentlessly. The Founding-25 clock starts at the gate (V2 done + app live + a few clients migrated clean), not before.
