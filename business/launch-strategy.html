01 Phase Overview

Four phases from internal beta to fitness studio expansion. Each phase has a clear focus and target segment.

Phase 0
Internal Beta
Pre-launch
BAM academies + consulting clients. High-volume feedback. Validate product across team sports, individual sports, and fitness before market launch.
Phase 1
Basketball Launch
Months 0–6
Leverage existing network. 100–200 basketball academies in first 90 days. Test traction in other team sports after month 3.
Phase 2
All Sports
Months 6–18
Pick 2–3 team sports. Create content, find partners, sponsor events. Goal: 300 non-basketball academies by Month 12.
Phase 3
Fitness
Months 18+
Fitness studios. More catered approach leveraging proven product and expanded operator network.

04 Positioning

Why FullControl, not the incumbent. The message that lands with academy owners in the first 30 seconds.

Current Tools (Them)
  • CoachIQ, Upper Hand, TeamSnap: tools that assist the business
  • Scheduling + payments, not automation
  • Generic — not built for trainers
  • No intelligence layer — just record-keeping
  • Still requires manual work for marketing, follow-up, retention
  • Multi-tool stack — no single source of truth
FullControl (Us)
  • Agents that run the business, not tools that assist it
  • Full stack: lead gen → cancellation
  • Built for basketball trainers — by one
  • Cross-domain intelligence — sees patterns across marketing, sales, retention
  • Automates the workflows you hate: follow-ups, no-shows, dunning, booking
  • One platform — replaces the stack
One-Line Pitch
"FullControl runs your basketball academy so you can spend time coaching, not managing."
Three Angles (Holy Trinity)
Time — get hours back from admin, follow-up, scheduling
Knowledge — know what's working, what's not, what to do next
Capital — more clients retained, more trials converted, lower churn
Target Archetypes
Young hungry trainer — wants to grow fast, build a brand, leverage tech
Established trainer — wants time back, family time, system running without them
