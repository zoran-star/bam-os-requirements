## 🎬 Show the magic (the wedge — lead with these)
- **"Talk to your business"** — say *"book makeups for everyone who missed Tuesday and text their parents,"* screen does it. The 15-second forwardable clip.
- **The Race** — split screen: old way (5 tabs, 5 min, timer) vs FullControl (one sentence).
- **"I texted my academy from bed"** — 11pm voice note → wake up to it done.
- **"What it did while I slept"** — morning dashboard reveal, time-lapse of overnight drafts.
- **"30 days, zero spreadsheets"** — Coleman challenge series.

## 👤 Founder / operator story (builds on founder-mission content)
- **"I built this because I lived the chaos"** — raw origin.
- **"We ran academies by hand for a year. Here's what we automated."** — credibility frame as a series.
- **"35 countries, one problem"** — operator-DNA origin film.
- **Build-in-public** — document replacing GHL, building the OS.

## 📅 Day-in-the-life (expands DITL)
- **DITL: owner with vs without FC** — transformation, same day, two timelines.
- **"A day in the life of your AI employee"** — personify the agent; shows it working 24/7.
- **DITL trainer** + the business quietly running itself behind them.

## 🧠 Teach the future (category creation)
- **"Your software was built by software people. Ours by operators."**
- **"The 7 tabs you'll never open again."**
- **"You don't need more leads. You need structure."** (myth-bust).
- **"All-in-one was always a lie — until AI made it real."**

## 🏀 Proof (dogfood + social proof)
- **BAM GTA = the public case study** — run your own academy on it, real before/after numbers.
- **"Founding 25" reveals** — profile each academy as they join (scarcity + proof).
- **Screen-recorded micro-testimonials** — 20-sec "the one thing it does for me."

## 🤝 Affiliates / creators (builds on Dcoop, TJL, Hennen, Danyliv)
- **"Run your academy on FC"** — documented series + rev-share; their audience watches the transformation.
- **"Roast my academy ops"** — creator submits chaos, you fix it on camera.
- **Co-branded "coaches who run it like pros."**

## 🎨 Renderings / brand films (builds on renderings concept)
- **"The cockpit for your academy"** — cinematic, lean into the bridge-console/aerospace brand.
- **Voice-wave → action** motion pieces.
- **Apple-style premium reveal** — one feature, beautifully, no clutter.

## 🚀 Launch stunts (bold / viral)
- **"I'll transform an academy in a week, free, on camera."**
- **Live "talk to your business" demo** at a coaching clinic / event.
- **Founding 25 countdown** — spots-left counter + "we're hand-picking 25" application.

## 📣 Organic BAM post (the angle)
- **"The business behind the basketball."** Pull back the curtain on how you actually run academies — operator authenticity, not a product ad. The product reveals itself naturally; won't feel salesy to the BAM audience.
