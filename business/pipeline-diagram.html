BAM GTA Sales Pipeline

GoHighLevel — 4 stages from form fill to trial completion

Stage 1
Interested
Auto-Entry
How They Get Here

Auto-moved on form fill: Free Trial Form, Contact Form, or Post Trial Form (missed trial trigger).

What's Happening

Every lead is placed into an automation sequence. Automated outreach is running — no lead sits idle in this stage.

Automations Active

New lead nurture sequence — multi-step SMS/email outreach to get a reply. Missed trial re-engagement — triggers for leads who came via post-trial form. All leads are in at least one active workflow.

Human Action

None. Fully automated. Automations handle all outreach until the lead responds.

How They Exit

Auto-moves to Responded when the lead replies to any message from this stage.

→
Stage 2
Responded
Auto-Entry
How They Get Here

Auto-moved when a lead replies from the Interested stage.

What's Happening

Live conversation is underway. Goal is to qualify the lead and book a free trial through one of the Free Trial Calendars.

Automations Active

Speed-to-lead notification — alerts staff when a lead responds. Stale conversation follow-up — re-engages if no reply after X hours. Conversation AI may be handling initial booking attempts.

Human Action

Engage in conversation. Answer questions, handle objections, and guide the lead toward booking a trial.

How They Exit

Auto-moves to Scheduled Trial when someone books through any of the Free Trial Calendars.

→
Stage 3
Scheduled Trial
Auto-Entry
How They Get Here

Auto-moved when a trial is booked through any Free Trial Calendar.

What's Happening

Trial is on the calendar. Lead is waiting for their scheduled session. Reminders and confirmations may be running.

Automations Active

Appointment reminders — confirmation + reminder SMS/email before the trial. No-show follow-up — triggers if the lead doesn't attend, attempts to rebook.

Human Action

Conduct the trial session. Deliver a great experience. After the trial, move the lead forward.

How They Exit

Manual move to Done Trial by staff after the trial session is completed.

→
Stage 4
Done Trial
Manual Entry
How They Get Here

Manually moved by staff from Scheduled Trial after the trial session happens.

What's Happening

Trial is complete. Lead is in decision mode — follow-up and closing efforts are active to convert them to a paying member.

Automations Active

Post-trial follow-up sequence — timed outreach to close the sale (pricing, testimonials, urgency). Closing AI — handles objections and nudges toward sign-up.

Human Action

Follow up and close. Send pricing, answer questions, handle objections, get them signed up.

How They Exit

Converts to member (payment collected, moved to member management) or marked lost.
