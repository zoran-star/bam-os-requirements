## The core analogy (say it exactly like this)

> **Waze:** every driver reports traffic → the map gets better for all drivers.
> **Global Brain:** every academy teaches the AI → the AI gets better for all academies.

**Punchline:** The only academy platform that gets smarter every time *any* academy uses it.
