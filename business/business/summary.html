Phase 1 · Own Basketball

We start where we have the most operator relationships. Through By Any Means Business, we’ve worked hands-on with 30+ basketball academies across North America and Australia, and Coleman and Mike’s combined network covers 150+ operator contacts across 40+ countries and 100+ cities.

Basketball is our proving ground full product depth across every business model a basketball academy could run:

  • Training classes & memberships
  • Team management
  • Gym rental
  • Internal leagues
  • External leagues under evaluation

Every feature, every AI workflow, every onboarding flow gets stress-tested against real academies first. We’re not cold-starting we’re productizing relationships that already exist.

Phase 2 · Expand into Sports Performance

Sports performance is our second-deepest network and the natural next step. Because performance facilities serve athletes from across every major sport under one roof, expanding here opens the door into every other sport at once and the feature investments we make for this vertical extend cleanly into the broader fitness industry.

Product coverage:

  • Training classes & memberships
  • Gym/facility rental
  • Player feedback & progression tracking
Phase 3 · Replicate into Other Sports & Fitness

Once the basketball and performance playbook is proven, the performance-trainer network hands us warm connections into every other sport soccer, baseball, volleyball, tennis, gymnastics, martial arts, and beyond which we use to launch vertical by vertical.

At the same scale, we extend into adjacent fitness industries that run on the same operational model as performance training. These adjacent verticals are a terminology and AI-prompt configuration on the same engine, not a rebuild.

We satisfy product-market fit in one vertical before touching the next, and let each prior market do the cross-industry marketing for free.
