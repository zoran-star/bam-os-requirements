Key GHL Primitives These Workflows Depend On

  • SMS sending — most workflows send SMS as the primary channel
  • Email sending — secondary channel, used heavily in onboarding
  • Reply detection — "responded to..." workflows all need to detect inbound replies to stop sequences
  • Delay/wait steps — timed delays between messages (minutes, hours, days, weeks)
  • Contact tagging — add/remove tags to categorize leads and trigger other workflows
  • Pipeline stage moves — move contacts through sales pipeline stages
  • Calendar/appointment triggers — fire on booking, completion, no-show
  • Form submission triggers — fire when specific forms are filled out
  • Payment event triggers — fire on successful/failed payments
  • Conditional branching — if/else logic based on tags, fields, time
  • Remove from workflow — stop a contact's position in another running workflow
  • Internal notifications — send staff alerts via SMS/email/in-app
